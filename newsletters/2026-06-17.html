🚀 SpaceX, Cursor, and the new AI coding economics
The reported $60B SpaceX-Cursor deal is really a compute, data, and distribution story. Cursor brings the developer workflow and enterprise footprint; SpaceX brings massive compute ambitions and a need to turn that capacity into durable revenue.
The lesson for teams is practical. When the harness steers model demand, vendor incentives matter. Kilo’s model-agnostic approach keeps your workflow flexible as compute, pricing, ownership, and model availability keep shifting underneath the market.

💸 The GitHub Copilot bill came due
Usage-based AI billing is no longer theoretical. Copilot’s June 1 pricing shift turned a line item many teams treated as fixed into a variable cost that moves with every chat, agent run, review, and tool call.
The real pain is predictability. Engineering leaders can plan around a higher bill; they cannot plan around a meter they cannot see or control. The answer is transparent usage, model choice, BYOK support, and governance before the overages start.

🎛️ Four levers for taking control of AI spend
The teams that win on AI spend will not be the ones that use agents less. They will be the ones that match the model to the job, see where tokens are going, govern usage across the org, and optimize the time and context agents burn along the way.
Kilo was built around those levers from day one. Over 500 models, usage analytics by model, project, and user, pooled credits, model access controls, Agent Manager worktrees, subagents, and codebase indexing all make cost control part of the workflow instead of a dashboard bolted on later.

🧭 You don’t have to use Fable and Mythos to work on the frontier
The frontier did not disappear when Fable and Mythos went dark. GPT-5.5, Nemotron 3 Ultra, MiniMax M3, and Kimi K2.7 Code all show how wide the model field has become, with serious options across frontier reasoning, open weights, cost efficiency, and tool use.
Reliability is a production feature. The most capable model you can use today is not always the most capable model you can depend on tomorrow. Kilo Gateway, BYOK, and model switching make resilience part of the platform.
