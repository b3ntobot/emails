📡 One session, every device
Remote sessions tie your local machine to the cloud dashboard. Turn on remote mode in the CLI and your active local sessions show up in the web interface next to your cloud sessions, with messages and responses syncing in real time.
Start something locally, check in from your phone, hand it off to a cloud session when you close the laptop. The agent's questions route to whichever device you have in front of you, and you can pick up mid-session without starting over. The compute stays on your machine, the access is everywhere.

💸 Powerful AI coding without the frontier-model bill
You can run Kilo well on $0/month — and even a paid setup doesn't have to hurt. A new guide from Brendan walks through three real budgets: $0, $5–15, and $20+, with the exact models and providers to use at each tier.
The headline move is Auto Efficient. It classifies each request in real time and routes to the cheapest model that KiloBench has proven accurate enough for that specific task. Routine work runs lean, hard work gets more firepower, and the router keeps adjusting as the session evolves. Auto Free is the zero-config path for $0.
